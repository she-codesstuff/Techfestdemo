About

Nagarjuna College (Nagarjuna College of Engineering & Technology - NCET) is a self-financing, autonomous engineering college established in 2001 under the Nagarjuna Education Society by Sri J.V. Ranga Raju. Located near Devanahalli, Bangalore, it is affiliated with VTU and approved by AICTE, offering B.E., M.Tech., and MBA programs. NCET is known for its industry-integrated curriculum, experienced faculty, on-campus residential facilities, and A+ NAAC accreditation with NIRF Innovation Ranking.
